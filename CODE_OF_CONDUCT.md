# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this to be a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of experience, background, or identity. Everyone interacting in this project's issues, pull requests, and discussions is expected to act with respect.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps:

- Being kind and assuming good intent.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for people using the project.

Examples of behavior that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unwelcome.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainer. Reports will be handled discreetly and in good faith. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this code, and may block repeat or serious offenders.
